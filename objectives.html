Objectives

  1. Provide tools and methods increasing capacity and facilitating Shared Situational Awareness of EU MS cybersecurity authorities.
  2. Provide tools and methods facilitating Coordinated Response between EU MS cybersecurity authorities and relevant EU entities at the technical and operational levels.
  3. Provide tools and methods facilitating Joint Preparedness for tackling large scale cyber-incidents, leveraging multi-faceted capacity building preparation activities and technologies, including MOOC training, Cyber Range hands-on training, and Cybersecurity Exercises.
  4. Provide mechanisms and processes enabling alerting, reporting, information exchange and collaboration at the technical and operational level between national and European actors.
  5. Integrate, demonstrate and validate the resulting platform through deployment at the Greek and Cypriot National Authorities, creating a Southeast Europe Coordinated Response Cluster.
