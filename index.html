    Joint Cybersecurity Operations Platform (JCOP)

    The project focuses on designing, developing, and delivering a Joint Cybersecurity Operations Platform tailored to the needs of EU member state authorities entrusted with cybersecurity and according to the guidelines provided in the EU Blueprint for coordinated response to large-scale cross-border cybersecurity incidents and crises - Recommendation C(2017)6100.
