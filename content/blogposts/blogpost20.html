SPHYNX Cyber Range in Action: The JCOP's Training Sessions

For the JCOP project, the SPHYNX Cyber Range platform was leveraged to deliver essential training sessions. SPHYNX developed additional content to enhance cybersecurity capabilities and delivered two separate training sessions: the first focused on Incident Response, while the second addressed GDPR-related topics.

These training sessions were part of the 2nd JCOP Capacity Building Workshop, organized in conjunction with the last JCOP Plenary Meeting in Athens on May 24, 2024. The event was a significant success, held both online and on-site at the Ministry of Digital Governance's facilities. It featured participants from the European Union Agency for Cybersecurity (ENISA), the National Cyber Security Authority (NCSA) of Greece, and the Digital Security Authority (DSA) of Cyprus.

Figure 1: Gathering at the Ministry of Digital Governance's facilities.

Delivered Training Programmes

Below is a description of each training programme delivered through the SPHYNX Cyber Range platform during the 2nd JCOP Capacity Building Workshop:

Incident Response Training Programme

The "Linux Tools in Incident Handling" programme empowers trainees with advanced skills in forensic analysis and attack trace discovery. Through a blend of theoretical instruction and practical exercises, participants learn to automate complex incident response tasks. The comprehensive hands-on assessment involves analyzing netflow logs to identify IP addresses, trace their origins, and extract critical attack information, ensuring a thorough understanding of cyber forensic techniques.

GDPR Training Programme

The GDPR training programme consists of two scenarios: “Introduction to GDPR” and “International Data Transfers.” The first scenario introduces the basic concepts of GDPR, its purposes, and applicability. The second scenario equips trainees with knowledge on cross-border data flows, covering topics like adequacy decisions, standard contractual clauses, and internal policies.

SPHYNX Cyber Range: Comprehensive Cybersecurity Training and Testing

In the rapidly evolving world of cybersecurity, continuous learning and practical experience are crucial. A Cyber Range (CR) is a controlled and secure environment designed for emulating, simulating, testing, and training cybersecurity defenses and responses. It provides a platform for users to practice defending computer systems, networks, and data against various cyber threats, including attacks, malware, and vulnerabilities.

The SPHYNX Cyber Range Platform is a cutting-edge solution for comprehensive cybersecurity training and testing. Key benefits and features of the SPHYNX CR platform include:

Training and Education: SPHYNX Cyber Range provides training and education in cybersecurity within a controlled environment for professionals, students, and employees to learn and practice their skills. The platform offers a range of training modules, from basic cybersecurity principles to advanced threat hunting and incident response.

Skill Development: The platform helps participants develop practical skills in identifying, mitigating, and responding to cyber threats through hands-on experience with various tools and techniques, ensuring users gain valuable, real-world skills.

Testing and Validation: Organizations can use SPHYNX CR to test and validate their cybersecurity defenses and incident response plans by simulating realistic cyberattacks to assess the effectiveness of security measures and response procedures.

Security Awareness: SPHYNX CR raises awareness about cybersecurity threats and best practices among employees and stakeholders through realistic training scenarios, helping individuals understand the potential consequences of cyberattacks and the importance of cybersecurity.

Customized Scenarios: Organizations can create customized training and testing scenarios that align with their specific needs and vulnerabilities, ensuring relevant and effective learning experiences.

Continuous Improvement: The platform supports ongoing learning and improvement in cybersecurity, with features for assessing participants' performance, tracking progress, and identifying areas for improvement.

Regulatory Compliance: SPHYNX CR assists organizations in meeting cybersecurity regulations and standards by providing a means to test and document security controls and incident response procedures.
